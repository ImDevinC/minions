## Codebase Patterns
<!-- Consolidate reusable patterns here -->
- Event content structure: messageID/part info can be at root, in `properties.part`, or in `part` directly - check all locations
- Use `Array.from(map.values())` instead of iterating `map.values()` directly to avoid TS downlevelIteration issues
- Delta tracking: use `DeltaState` with both `textByPart` map and `processedEventIds` set to avoid double-counting on re-aggregation
- Subtask linking: task tool `callID` becomes the subtask's sessionID; filter events by sessionID to group subtask conversations
